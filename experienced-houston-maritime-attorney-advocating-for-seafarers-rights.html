The Importance of Maritime Law

Maritime law, also known as admiralty law, is a specialized field that governs activities on navigable waters. An experienced Houston maritime attorney plays a vital role in advocating for the rights of seafarers, ensuring they receive the legal protection and support they need in the event of accidents or injuries at sea.
