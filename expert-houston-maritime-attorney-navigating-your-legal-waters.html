Understanding Maritime Law

Maritime law, also known as admiralty law, governs activities on navigable waters. This area of law covers a wide range of issues, including shipping, navigation, and marine commerce. It also addresses personal injury claims arising from maritime activities. An expert Houston maritime attorney specializes in these legal matters, ensuring that clients receive the representation they need.
